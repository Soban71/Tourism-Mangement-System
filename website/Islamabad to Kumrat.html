Places We Visit in Kumrat Valley

Jahaz Banda Meadows

Jahaz Banda is a large meadow located in the upper reaches of the Valley, Upper Dir District of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa Province of Pakistan. The meadow located at an altitude of 3,100 meters above sea level. Can are many snow-clad mountains, green pastures, and many high towering trees that can see in the Jahaz Banda.

Wadi-e-Kumrat

All the above mentioned places have also come in the sight of tourists when the beauty of Kumrat valley has attracted the tourists. Some people have started remembering this valley as the queen of valleys which is the best address according to the beauty of this valley.

Panjkora River

Jandri Village is a village located in the Karak District of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, Pakistan. It is also a Union Council in Karak. The village located at an altitude of 74 meters. It is one of the best places to visit if you are coming to Kumrat Valley. Famous for its excellent sweet homes, tourists and travellers visit this village because of excellent views, cold but pleasant weather, and meeting the people who live in the village.

Bara Dand Lake

Bara Dand Lake is a lake located in the valley in the Upper Dir District of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, Pakistan. The lake is very famous for its flora and fauna. The fantastic view is that freezing but pleasant weather and icy-cold water make the lake more special for tourists and travellers. Many tourists and travellers of the valley visit Bara Dand Lake every year and take the best experience.
