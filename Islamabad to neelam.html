Places We Visit in Neelam Valley

Kel and Arrankel

Kel-and-Arrang-Kel This is followed by Kel and Arrang Kel, one of the attractions in the Neelum Valley. One of the facts is that there is still a long way to go to Nanga Parbat. People can really see Nanga Parbat Mountain from there, and it is a beautiful view for everyone who visits it. Only a 3-hour stroll from Kel to Arrang Kel.

Dhani Waterfall

The next one on the list is Dhani Waterfall, about 30 kilometres from the provincial capital Muzaffarabad. Dhani’s pure crystal-clear water is one of the most stunning waterfalls in the area, attracting thousands of tourists. March to mid-June is the best time to visit Dhani waterfall is from.

Rati Gali Lake

The lake can be reached through Dowarian, a small town in the Neelum Valley, famous for its picturesque beauty. You cannot miss any view of the magnificent lake because the waters are so unique that they cannot reflect the surrounding environment. The area is very mild and peaceful, and people can walk to the most charming part of the Neelum Valley

Sharda Neelum Valley

. Tourists like to walk on the lush green lawn. Sharda also has a very rich historical background, which enhances the curiosity of tourists who come here once in a lifetime. Sharda Neelum valley has various tourist breaks, you can drink a cup of coffee or live in the closest place to nature.
